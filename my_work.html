What I currently do

Maintainer and associate of QueroElo!.

QueroElo!

QueroElo! is a game services web-store built using the Python Django Framework. In conjunction to it, there is a system to keep track of all hired personnel, as well as the way the team comunicates and staff members get job assigments, it is fully integrated with Slack and handles payments via the PicPay REST API. I sketched, built, deployed and maintain the complete project.
